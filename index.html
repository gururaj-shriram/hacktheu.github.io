UHack 2017

UHack is a 24 hour hackathon hosted by the University of Miami. It will be held at the University of Miami campus ballroom in the Shalala Center. It is completely FREE for anyone in the area, and will be fully equipped with any snacks and drinks you may need to keep you going throughout the night. Come build the future!

Frequently Asked Questions


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a weekend long event where you invent the future: from idea to prototype. At the end of UHack, you will pitch your prototype. And of courses, there are prizes. Hackathons are a great place to learn quick and create something awesome.


How much does it cost?

UHack is completely free for all registered participants! The food is free and all the awesome swag is free.


Will there be food?

Absolutely! We four delicious meals planned with plenty of snacks in between to keep you going for 24 hours.


Is there any travel reimbursement?

Unfortunately, we will not be offering individual travel reimbursement this year.


What should I bring?

Bring you, your computer (chargers and any other hacking gear you need), and toiletries (toothpaste, toothbrush, etc).


Help, this is my first hackathon!?

No matter your major or skill level, you're more than welcome at UHack! We will have workshops during the event and mentors to help you through any troubles. So no matter what skill levels, we will provide the resources to help you learn.


Can high school students attend?

Yes! If you are attending a Miami-Dade high school, we're super psyched to have you.
